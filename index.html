About 6ox

It's all about 6ox, a simple platformer game that was made in a single day

Background
In the continually evolving digital era, the gaming industry has become one of the most intriguing and profitable sectors. Technological advancements and the popularity of video games have generated a high demand for innovative, engaging, and entertaining games. This can be observed from the data in (Most Gamers in Indonesia Ranked Third in the World, n.d.), indicating that Indonesia holds the third position out of 10 countries with the highest number of video game players in the world in 2022. This illustrates the significant interest in video games in Indonesia across various demographics. In the midst of an increasingly globally connected society, games have become a powerful tool for entertainment, inspiration, and connecting people from diverse cultural and social backgrounds. In other words, games have become a common activity for everyone, utilizing various devices that suit each individual. We have created a simple puzzle game called "6ox." This game provides a enjoyable, challenging, and captivating gaming experience, inviting players to think critically in order to overcome various challenges and achieve their goals. The game is designed to be playable by individuals of all ages, including children and adults. With straightforward gameplay and challenging level layouts, we hope to create an addictive and entertaining game.
Mechanics and Player's Role
Players will take on the role of a red-colored square that can move and jump freely, as well as push other flat structures to solve puzzles. Players must utilize logic and problem-solving skills to navigate through the mechanisms, opening paths to hexagonal portals and overcoming obstacles at each level. The game has a time limit of 60 seconds for each level, and if the time runs out, players must restart from the beginning. The 60-second timer resets with each attempt. Players can jump off walls to reach higher areas by pressing the spacebar when touching the wall.
High Concept Statement
"6ox" is a simple puzzle game application that challenges players to jump, push, and perform various actions with flat structures to complete levels filled with creative puzzles. In this adventure journey, players will explore a world filled with diverse flat structures with the aim of reaching the portal.
